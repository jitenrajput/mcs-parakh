## Architecture
**M-Connect Labs** = team · **MCS Parakh** = product (deck title, README, submission) · **Parakh** = what every screen says. Footer: *"built for IDBI Innovate 2026"* — never "in partnership with"; never IDBI logo/colors as if endorsed.

## Story & taglines
Parakh = assaying gold on a touchstone; the hallmark metaphor unifies name, logo, tagline, and score chips.
Hindi: **"Parakh — har khare vyapar ki pehchaan"** · English: **"Every genuine business, recognized."**

## Product family

## Name hygiene
NCERT's "PARAKH" exists (education sector) — MCS prefix is the protection; no uniqueness claims; class-36 trademark search post-hackathon. Juror line: "ours assays businesses, not students."

## Anti-generic UI rules (LOCKED Jul 06 — JR: "no identifiable AI-built design")
1. **Banned:** purple/indigo, gradients, glassmorphism, emoji in product UI, "empower/unlock" copy, 4-KPI-cards-default dashboards.
2. **Trilingual type as signature:** Mukta + Noto Sans across EN/Devanagari/Gujarati, tabular ₹ figures.
3. **Metaphor-driven components:** assay-stamp chips, coverage ring, jeweler's-balance gauge, SYNTHETIC watermark as rotated rubber bank-stamp — never library defaults.
4. **Domain texture:** ₹1,23,456 + ₹19.2L grouping (never ₹1.9M), monospace GSTIN/IFSC, DD-MM-YYYY, credit-memo vocabulary.
5. **Layout against the grain:** lender side dense (credit-memo/terminal), MSME side warm — the contrast IS the design.
6. **Real personas on every screen from first render** — no placeholders, no lorem.
7. **Review test per screen:** "Would a credit officer recognize this as built for their world?" Could-be-any-SaaS = fail. First rendered screenshots go to JR+JT for the AI-smell-test before polish.
