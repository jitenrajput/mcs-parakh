# MCS Parakh (परख)

**AI-driven MSME Financial Health Card — built for IDBI Innovate 2026 (PS3: Financial Health Score) by Team M-Connect Labs.**

> *Parakh — har khare vyapar ki pehchaan.* Recognizing every genuine business.

India's 63M MSMEs face a ₹25–30 lakh crore credit gap; only ~14% access formal credit. Scores exist, rails exist — what's missing is the **bank-side product layer** that lets a lender actually act on GST, UPI, Account Aggregator and EPFO data. Parakh is that layer: a **two-sided, explainable, continuously-monitored Financial Health Card**.

## What it does

- **Lender view**: 0–900 composite health score across 5 explainable dimensions (Cash-Flow Strength, Compliance Discipline, Growth Trajectory, Obligation Load, Business Stability), SHAP-based reason codes, data-completeness confidence, product-propensity suggestions (OD / Term Loan / Trade Finance), CGTMSE eligibility flag, and portfolio early-warning alerts.
- **MSME view**: the business sees its own Health Card, plain-language strengths & risks, and concrete "improve your score" actions — financial inclusion built into the UX.

## Architecture

```
[Data adapters (mock for PoC)]      [Core engine]                  [Clients]
 GST returns  ─┐          FeatureExtractor (per source)      React web app
 AA bank data ─┼────────► ScoringOrchestrator (pluggable)    ├─ Lender dashboard
 UPI flows    ─┤          Explainability (SHAP → reasons)    └─ MSME self-view
 EPFO         ─┘          FastAPI REST (ULI-ready OpenAPI)
```

**PoC honesty note:** all data sources are synthetic (generated by `backend/datagen/`), following public AA/GST schema conventions. The adapter layer is designed to swap in IDBI's sandbox APIs in the prototype phase.
